"ADONIS NP" is a Business Process Management (BPM) and Analysis (BPA) tool. It supports business process management based on the BPMS framework created at the University of Vienna. The tool has been developed by BOC Information Technologies Consulting GmbH. It provides an array of functionalities, including, but not limited to web-based business process modeling using the BPMS notation and BPMN 2.0 , search and graphical analysis capabilities, process simulation & optimization, evaluation, as well as publishing and process automation with BPMN 2.0 XML (BPMN DI) and XPDL.
ADONIS NP exists in multiple different variations, and apart from its commercial editions (ADONIS NP Enterprise & Starter Editions), a freeware ADONIS "Community Edition" is also available.
