"FASTER" is a trans-Pacific submarine communications cable that went live during the last week of June 2016. The cable has a total length of approximately 11,629&nbsp;km and a capacity of 60Tb/s.

The companies involved in the project include:
* Google
* KDDI
* SingTel
* China Telecom Global
* China Mobile International
* Global Transit Communications

FASTER has landings in:
* Bandon, Oregon, USA
* Chikura, Chiba, Japan
* Shima, Mie, Japan
* Tamsui, New Taipei City, Taiwan
